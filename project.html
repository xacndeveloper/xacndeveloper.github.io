// about the project

This is a fan-made remake project developed with the Godot Engine. It is currently in the early stages of development (Work in Progress), focusing heavily on atmosphere, mechanics, and core gameplay.

Instead of a rushed map, every spotlight and light source has been placed manually, one by one, to ensure a high-quality, atmospheric, and tense environment.

// Current Features

Advanced FPS Controller
Smooth and responsive movement controls.
Immersive Camera Mechanics
Realistic camera tilts (leaning) and camera shake effects that react to your movement.
Realistic Flashlight
A dynamic flashlight with realistic lighting and shadows to navigate the darkness.
Granny AI
The core enemy AI is active and ready to hunt you down.

// What's Coming Next?

Since the project is only 5–6 days old, many features are still being worked on. In future updates, I will be adding:

Full map navigation and layout.
Hiding mechanics (such as hiding under beds).
Item systems, puzzles, and escape mechanics.

Feel free to follow the project and leave your feedback as I continue to develop.
